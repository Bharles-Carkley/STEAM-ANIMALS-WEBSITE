Threats

There are many threats to the beloved Snow Leopard and our organisation does our best to prevent these Snow Leopards from being victim to endangerment. The sole predator of snow leopards are Humans, from poaching, habitat loss and decrease in prey, we are the reason for the vulnreability of these cats. The other big impact is climate change, the rising temperatures severely impact their habitats in the high mountains where temperatures are supposed to remain cold, climate change has caused 30%+ in the loss of snow leopard habitats. To stop these threats we must take Action, this includes donating to organisations dedicated to preventing animals such as the snow leopard from extinction. We also must act socially, we need to erradicate poaching, we need to know what we are destroying before we do it to stop habitat loss and we need to combat climate change as a whole.
